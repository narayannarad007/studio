# **App Name**: CareerPilot AI

## 🚀 Core Features (Launch Version):

- **AI-Powered Job Analysis**: Parse job descriptions from URLs or pasted text, calculate an AI match score, identify matched/missing skills, and generate actionable improvement suggestions.
- **Resume & Profile Management**: Upload, parse, and store PDF/DOCX resumes. Maintain a structured profile utilized for all AI insights.
- **Tailored Application Generator**: Generate job-specific resume suggestions and personalized cover letters with tone selection.
- **AI-Driven Interview Prep**: Generate likely interview questions (behavioral, technical) directly from job descriptions with AI guidance.
- **Interactive AI Career Assistant**: Context-aware chat for resume advice, search strategies, and skill development.
- **Comprehensive Application Tracker**: Dashboard to save jobs, track status (Saved, Applied, Interview), and manage follow-ups.
- **Browser Extension Support**: Seamless job import directly from job boards.

## 💰 Monetization & Security:

- **India-First Subscription Model**: Premium "Pro" plan at ₹299/month, optimized for the Indian market via Razorpay.
- **Server-Side Quota Enforcement**: Atomic credit tracking for AI flows (Job Analysis, Resumes, Cover Letters, etc.) to control API costs.
- **Usage Dashboard**: Real-time "Usage Meter" for users to track their monthly limits and upgrade status.
- **Integrated Payments**: Secure checkout flow with UPI, Card, and Netbanking support.

## 🏁 Launch Readiness:

- **Guided Onboarding**: Mission-style checklist on the dashboard to help new users achieve their first "AHA" moment (AI Analysis).
- **Compliance & Legal**: Professional Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and Support pages to ensure trust and operational transparency.
- **Launch Support**: Dedicated Help Center and Support contact at support@careerpilotai.com.
